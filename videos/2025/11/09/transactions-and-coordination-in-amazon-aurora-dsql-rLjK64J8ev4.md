## Description

Marc Brooker, VP and Distinguished Engineer at AWS, discusses Aurora DSQL, a new global, serverless, scalable relational database system developed at AWS. He explains the system's architecture, how it handles transactions, and its design focus on minimizing coordination. Additionally, he covers transaction protocols, isolation, and virtualization.
